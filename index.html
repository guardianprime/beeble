We help connect workers create their own tools for their daily activities. With these products our customers, create amazing and unique things
Beeble was created on september 27 2023 as just a practice sitebut later it turned out to become a bigshot tech startup because of the high demand for such services. It became a dream that could not be dimmed.
Working everyday at our various instutions and crafts can be some times hectic without the right tools to soften our burdens
That is why here at bimble we created a set of surated tools that can make things much easier. The driving force is our passion for what we make and the people we make it for.
Our staff comprises of more than a thousand poeple from 1oo+ countries, but our main office is in Lagos, Nigeria.
Most of us hear at bimble are active tradepersons, vocationists and poeple from different walks of life that technology has not deeply influenced. some of us have Phds, some are self taught, some of us went to vocations chools and community colleges. The binding glue is the shared passion for making our daily lives as employees and self-employed persons easier.
We believe it takes focus to create truly outstanding instruments. We only work on a few products and we strive to make them great.
Rather than creating a jack of all trade application, instead we delved deeply into every software we have created here on our platform. They are all tailored to fit exactly the usecase needed by our customers. Sometimes we don't always agree with eachother, but opinion and debate are valued and openly encouraged.
We are very proud of what we create, but we are also open to evolve and improve who we are.
All our employees work very hard to make and create a fertile environment for every idea brought by you and us to grow very richly.
Alongside our quaterly training program we also actively support our emlpoyees to learn and acquire new skills. in addition self-development and new technologies are also highly apprecitated,
